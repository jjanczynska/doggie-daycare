Our Story

Welcome to Islanders Doggie Day Care, a sanctuary for your four-legged family members right here on our idyllic Atlantic Island off the coast of Ireland. Our journey started with a challenge that's all too familiar to local pet parents. Our founder, Joanna, was a devoted dog mom to Freya, her charming English Springer Spaniel. Living amidst the natural splendors of the island, Joanna cherished long beach strolls and woodland hikes with Freya. But balancing a busy work schedule with Freya's need for exercise and companionship became increasingly taxing. Search as she might, Joanna found no local doggie day care that met her high standards of safety, engagement, and loving care. Inspired by her own struggle, Joanna founded Islanders Doggie Day Care to provide a nurturing environment for pets and peace of mind for their owners. Here, safety, happiness, and well-being are not mere words but our foundational principles. Our facility boasts secure, clean, and enriching spaces designed to keep your dog engaged and elated. We offer tailored activities that stimulate your pup's body and mind.
